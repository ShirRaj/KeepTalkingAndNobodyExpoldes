Events protocol:

MUST start with "HOST"

format: "HOST ACTION KEY:VAL,KEY:VAL...."
*no limit on KEY:VAL
**KEY:VAL optional
* + ** depends on ACTION

posible actions:

1. BOOM, no params
2. ALL_SOLVED, no params
3. SOLVED ID:module_id
4. WIRES COLORS:c0,c1,...,c5 (ci color acording to Kenneth protocol, by the order from left to right)
5. READY/UNREADY
